FAQ's about National Parks

Where is National Park with the largest cave system?
Mammoth National Park in Kentucky is the longest recorded cave system in the world, more than 336 miles.

Which national park protects the tallest mountain in the contiguous 48 states?
Sequoia and Kings Canyon National Parks

What Spanish explorer is credited with discovering the Grand Canyon in 1540?
Garcia Lopez de Cardenas

What feature is unique about Grand Canyon rattlesnakes?
They are pink so they blend in with the reddish dirt of the canyon.

What is the southernmost U.S. national park?
National Park of American Samoa

Which national park protects the tallest volcano in the lower 48?
Mount Rainier National Park

When was the most recent volcanic eruption within Yellowstone park?
600,000 years ago,It spewed out nearly 240 cubic miles of debris and a 28 by 47-mile caldera was formed.

What is the largest national park site?
Wrangell-St. Elias National Park and Preserve, AK, at 13.2 million acres

Smallest?
Smallest: Thaddeus Kosciuszko National Memorial, PA, at 0.02 acres

Where is the worlds largest spring fed swimming pool?
In Balmorhea State Park.
